About 500 Startups Vietnam

500 Startups Vietnam is a $10M micro-VC fund of 500 Startups focused specifically on investing in and supporting the growth of promising Vietnamese startups as well as the overall Vietnamese startup ecosystem. We invest in both global and regional focused startups. We help globally focused companies through our Silicon Valley based accelerator program and like working alongside talented local investors for regionally focused startups.

We believe Vietnam can become the technology hub for Southeast Asia. We’re working hard - like the talented entrepreneurs we invest in - to help make that happen. We look forward to working with you.
